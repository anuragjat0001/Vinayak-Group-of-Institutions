Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Education & Community

Vinayak Group of Education was established in 1995 with the founding of Vinayak Vidhya Vihar Senior Secondary School in Chomu. Built on a simple yet powerful belief — that quality education should be accessible to every student — the Group has grown from a single institution into a complete educational ecosystem over three decades.

Today, the Group comprises seven institutions spanning CBSE-affiliated English-medium schools, co-educational Hindi-medium secondary schooling, degree colleges for men and women, and a dedicated NCTE-approved teacher training institution. Every institution is designed to serve the students and families of Chomu and the broader Jaipur district.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To nurture responsible, skilled, and knowledge-driven citizens who contribute meaningfully to society — through accessible, quality education at every stage of life, rooted in values of integrity, discipline, and community service.

Our Mission

To deliver structured academic programmes across school and higher education levels; to maintain rigorous standards of teaching and learning; to support the holistic development of every student; and to continuously serve the evolving educational needs of Rajasthan's communities.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

From a single school to seven institutions — a story of steady growth driven by community trust.

1995

The Foundation

Vinayak Vidhya Vihar Senior Secondary School is established in Chomu City — the founding institution of the Vinayak Group, offering Hindi-medium co-educational schooling from Class 1 to 12 under the Rajasthan Board.

2003

Entering Higher Education

Vinayak PG College is established, marking the Group's entry into degree-level education. Affiliated to the University of Rajasthan, Jaipur, the college begins offering undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Arts, Science, and Commerce.

2005

English-Medium Schooling

Vinayak International School opens at Khadi Bagh, Chomu — a CBSE-affiliated English-medium school with boarding facilities, managed by Vinayak Shri Krishan Sansthan, spanning 2.29 acres.

2007

Empowering Women's Education

Vinayak Girls College is established on Kacholiya Road — a dedicated degree college for girls offering UG and PG programmes in Arts, Science, and Commerce, with a sanctioned intake of 820 seats.

2013

Expanding Higher Education

Vinayak International College opens at Krishana Vihar, Kacholiya Road, providing additional UG and PG capacity in Arts, Science, and Commerce for students of the Chomu region.

2018

Second CBSE Campus

A second campus of Vinayak International School is opened on Kacholiya Road — spanning 4,000 sq.m., offering Nursery to Class XII with a rich co-curricular programme including NCC, Scouts, Drama, and more.

2021

Professional Teacher Training

Shri Vinayak College of Education is established — a dedicated B.Ed institution approved by NCTE, New Delhi and the Government of Rajasthan, completing the Group's full educational pathway from primary school to professional teacher training.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Academic Integrity

All institutions are affiliated with recognised statutory bodies — CBSE, University of Rajasthan, NCTE. Academic quality and compliance are central to everything we do.

Holistic Development

Education extends beyond the classroom. Physical fitness, creative expression, social responsibility, and character building are equally part of the Vinayak experience.

Community Service

The Vinayak Group was built in Chomu, for Chomu. Providing quality education to the families of this tehsil and the broader Jaipur district remains our primary responsibility.

Inclusive Access

Co-educational and girls-only institutions, Hindi and English-medium schools, and day and boarding facilities ensure education is accessible to every student we serve.

Recognised Standards

From CBSE board schools to NCTE-approved B.Ed programmes, every academic offering meets the standards set by national regulatory and statutory bodies.

Continuous Growth

From one institution in 1995 to seven by 2021 — the Group's consistent expansion reflects an unwavering commitment to meeting the growing educational needs of the region.
