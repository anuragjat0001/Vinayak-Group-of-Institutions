Infrastructure

Facilities at a Glance

Vinayak Group institutions are equipped with infrastructure that complements academic programmes and supports student wellbeing.

Library & Reading Room

A well-stocked library with a dedicated reading room for quiet individual study, supporting academic research and exam preparation at Vinayak PG College.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped labs supporting practical coursework in Physics, Chemistry, Botany, and Zoology for UG and PG students at Vinayak PG College.

Computer Lab

Individual computer systems with internet access enabling students to utilise digital resources and conduct online research at Vinayak PG College.

Multipurpose Hall

A spacious hall at Vinayak PG College suitable for seminars, cultural programmes, examinations, orientation events, and institutional gatherings.

Sports, Yoga & Training

Outdoor sports ground, Yoga sessions, Scout & Guide programmes, and military training at Vinayak PG College for holistic physical development.

Boarding Facilities

Residential boarding facilities at both campuses of Vinayak International School providing a safe and structured environment for boarding students.

Canteen

An on-campus canteen at Vinayak PG College provides students convenient access to food and refreshments during the academic day.

Health Centre

Basic medical assistance and first-aid support available on campus at Vinayak PG College for the wellbeing of students and staff.

Online Fee Payment

Vinayak Girls College provides QR-code-based online fee payment on its official website for convenient digital transactions by families.

Vinayak PG College — In Depth

College Infrastructure Details

Academic Resources

Library & Reading Room

The college library houses a wide collection of textbooks, reference books, and study materials covering all subjects offered across UG and PG programmes. A dedicated Reading Room provides students with a structured, distraction-free environment for focused study and examination preparation.

Wide collection — textbooks, references, journals across Arts, Science and Commerce

Separate Reading Room for individual quiet study

Accessible to all enrolled UG and PG students

Practical Learning

Science Laboratories

The college maintains well-equipped science laboratories that support hands-on practical learning and university-mandated practical examinations. Labs are aligned with the curriculum requirements of the University of Rajasthan for B.Sc. and M.Sc. programmes.

Physics lab with standard instruments and apparatus

Chemistry lab for both UG and PG practical sessions

Botany & Zoology labs supporting all life science programmes

Digital Infrastructure

Computer Lab

The computer laboratory is equipped with individual workstations connected to the internet, enabling students to access digital learning platforms, conduct research, submit online forms, and develop computing skills relevant to their academic and professional development.

Individual computer systems with internet connectivity

Supports online research, digital resources, and scholarship portal access

Available to all UG and PG students of the college

Holistic Development

Sports, Yoga & Physical Training

Vinayak PG College integrates physical fitness as a core pillar of student development. Beyond outdoor sports, the college runs structured programmes in Yoga, Scout & Guide training, and military training — instilling discipline, resilience, and physical wellbeing alongside academic achievement.

Outdoor sports ground for group athletics and fitness activities

Regular Yoga sessions for mental and physical wellness

Scout & Guide and military training as structured physical programmes
