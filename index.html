About Vinayak Group

A Legacy of Education
Built in Chomu

Vinayak Group of Education has been serving the students and families of Chomu and the surrounding Jaipur district since 1995. From a single school, the Group has grown into a complete educational ecosystem spanning CBSE schools, degree colleges, and professional training institutions.

Our institutions are affiliated with CBSE and the University of Rajasthan, Jaipur, and hold approvals from NCTE, New Delhi and the Government of Rajasthan — ensuring every programme meets recognised national academic standards.
