01 Senior Secondary School · RBSE

Vinayak Vidhya Vihar Senior Secondary School

Est. 1995

The founding institution of Vinayak Group, Vinayak Vidhya Vihar Senior Secondary School has been serving the Chomu community since 1995. This co-educational Hindi-medium school provides structured education from pre-primary through Class XII under the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education.

Managed as a private unaided institution, the school features a pucca building, eight well-maintained classrooms, all-weather road access, and an electricity connection — providing a conducive learning environment for students across all grades.

07 B.Ed Teacher Training · NCTE Approved

Shri Vinayak College of Education

Est. 2021

The Group's most recently established institution, Shri Vinayak College of Education is a dedicated teacher training college offering the 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) programme. Fully approved by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), New Delhi, and the Government of Rajasthan, and affiliated to the University of Rajasthan, Jaipur.

Admission is through the official PTET (Pre Teacher Education Test) Counseling process conducted by the Government of Rajasthan. Fees are charged as per Government of Rajasthan norms, making this an accessible option for aspiring teachers.
