UG & PG Programmes

Degree College Admission Process

Applicable for Vinayak PG College, Vinayak Girls College, and Vinayak International College.

1

Check Eligibility

For UG (B.A., B.Sc., B.Com.): 10+2 pass from a recognised board.
For PG (M.A., M.Sc., M.Com.): Graduation in the relevant subject from a recognised university.

2

Collect Application Form

Obtain the application form from the respective college office or through the official website — vinayakpg.com (Vinayak PG College) or vinayakgirlscollege.in (Vinayak Girls College).

3

Submit Documents

Submit the completed form along with attested copies of all required documents at the college office. See the document checklist below.

4

Merit List Declaration

Admission is primarily merit-based on qualifying examination marks. The college publishes a merit list. Students are called for document verification in order of merit.

5

Fee Payment

Pay the prescribed fee at the college accounts section or through the online fee portal. Vinayak Girls College also offers QR-code-based online payment on its official website.

6

Enrollment Confirmation

After successful fee payment, collect your enrollment number and student ID card. Your admission to the programme is officially confirmed at this stage.

Checklist

Documents Required

Keep attested photocopies of the following documents ready at the time of admission.

Class 10th Marksheet & Certificate
Class 12th Marksheet & Certificate
Graduation Marksheet (for PG programmes)
Transfer Certificate (TC)
Character Certificate
Caste Certificate (if applicable)
Passport-size Photographs (4–6 copies)
Aadhaar Card / Government ID Proof
Migration Certificate (if applicable)
Income Certificate (for scholarship/fee waiver)
Teacher Training

B.Ed Admission Process

Applicable for Shri Vinayak College of Education — NCTE Approved, University of Rajasthan Affiliated.

1

Appear for PTET

Register for and appear in the PTET (Pre Teacher Education Test) conducted annually by the Government of Rajasthan. This is a mandatory qualifying examination.

2

Obtain Qualifying Rank

Score a qualifying rank in PTET. Eligibility requires Graduation with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognised university. The rank determines counseling priority.

3

Participate in PTET Counseling

Participate in the official PTET Counseling process organised by the Government of Rajasthan. Choose Shri Vinayak College of Education at the time of option filling.

4

Seat Allotment & Confirmation

Upon seat allotment to Shri Vinayak College of Education, complete document verification and pay the prescribed fee as per Government of Rajasthan norms.

Note: Fee for the B.Ed programme is charged strictly as per Government of Rajasthan norms. For PTET registration and exam dates, refer to the official Rajasthan PTET portal or contact Shri Vinayak College of Education directly.

School Level

School Admission Process

Applicable for all three Vinayak Group schools — Vinayak Vidhya Vihar School and both Vinayak International School campuses.

1

Collect Admission Form

Obtain the admission form from the school office. Enquire about availability of seats in the desired grade before collecting the form.

2

Submit Documents

Submit the completed form along with a birth certificate, previous class marksheet/report card, Transfer Certificate, and passport-size photographs.

3

Admission Test / Interaction

The school may conduct an admission test or parent-child interaction session depending on the grade applied for. This helps in appropriate grade placement.

4

Confirmation & Fee Payment

Admission is confirmed on a merit basis. Pay the fee at the school accounts office to complete enrollment and receive the student ID and timetable.
